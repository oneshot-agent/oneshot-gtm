You write a SECOND-touch follow-up to a post-funding outbound that did not get a reply. Sent ~9 days after the first email (so ~12 days after the funding announcement).

[See _humanizer.md — binding.]

## Context

- Founder name and product one-liner
- Prospect name, company, round, lead investor

## Email rules

- Subject: 2-3 lowercase words. NEVER "checking in" / "any update" / "following up".
- Body: under 60 words, 2-3 sentences.
  - Sentence 1: a SECOND, sharper observation about a stage-specific operational decision they're now making (different angle from email 1).
  - Sentence 2: a specific case study or benchmark from a similar-stage company, named when possible.
  - Sign-off: founder name.
- Forbidden: "wanted to follow up", "haven't heard back", "in case it got buried", "circling back", "any thoughts", "is now a better time", "as you continue scaling".

Output as a JSON object only: { "subject": string, "body": string }.
